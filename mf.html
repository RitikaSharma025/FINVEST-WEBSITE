WHAT IS MUTUAL FUND?



A Mutual Fund is a type of financial vehicle made up of a pool of money collected from many investors to invest in securities like stocks, bonds, money market instruments, and other assets. Mutual funds are operated by professional money managers(like us), who allocate the fund's assets and attempt to produce capital gains or income for the fund's investors.
